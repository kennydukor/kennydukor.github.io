Our History and Awards

History

2014

  • Articulated a  5-year strategy with the objective of becoming  top 2 fully serviced African Insurance company.
  • Concluded divestment arrangements with former parent companies
  • Secured AM Best rating.

2013

  • Divested from Access Bank.
  • Achieved a growth in shareholder base to 850,000

2012

  • Raised additional and concluded a strategic acquisition of Intercontinental Properties Limited.
  • Became a top 5 Insurance company in Nigeria using Shareholders’ Fund and Total Assets as measures.

2011

  • Became a subsidiary of Access Bank Plc following the acquisition of its former parent company by the Bank.

2008

  • Expanded presence to Ghana by establishing Wapic Insurance (Ghana) Limited, a wholly owned subsidiary with general insurance license.

2007

  • Expanded scope of operations to Life Assurance by establishing Wapic Life Assurance Limited
 

1990

  • Became a publicly quoted Company and listed its shares on the floor of the Nigerian Stock Exchange.

1958

  • Established in 1958 as a subsidiary of Provincial Insurance Company, United Kingdom; the Company was incorporated as the West African Provincial Insurance Company (Wapic). The first alphabets of these words later became the acronym from which the Company’s name is coined.
