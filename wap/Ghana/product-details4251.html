    MOOV (Third Party)

    It is a simple insurance product which covers the policyholder against legal liability to third parties in the event of death, bodily injury and property damage involving the insured vehicle. Third party insurance is a mandatory requirement for all vehicles on Nigerian roads. It became mandatory through the Motor Vehicle (third party) Insurance Act of 1945.

    Features

    • A flat rate payable annually to insure a vehicle against all third party liabilities (subject to policy conditions)
    • The policy will still operate if you use your vehicle to tow another broken down vehicle provided you're not being paid to do so
    • Simple premium calculations

    Benefits

    • Pays third party property damage up to GH¢ 3,000.00
    • Unlimited third party death and bodily injury
    • Personal Accident Cover of GH¢ 2,000 for the driver

    To enjoy these benefits?

    • Complete the application form
    • Effect premium payment
    • Observe the terms and conditions outlined in the policy document.

    GET A QUOTE

    Documentation

    • Completed proposal form
    • Proof of ownership
    • Vehicle License
    • Acceptable proof of identification

    Exclusions and Limitations

    • Own damage, fire & theft claims are not covered
    • No provision for accidental bodily injury benefits for policyholder/driver
    • Medical expenses for anyone injured in your vehicle are excluded
    • The policy will not pay for bodily injury or damage to the property of any members of your household
    • No cover for accidents happening outside the stated geographical area (Nigeria)
    • Usual exclusions apply to war, nuclear risks and volcanic eruption
    • An excess of 10% of claim amount or N2,000 whichever is greater applies to third party property damage
    • If at the point of making a claim, it is discovered that the vehicle is also insured by other insurance companies,the cost of claim will be shared with the other companies
    • Loss of use or consequential losses

    Switching between policy covers

    Yes you can. With the Moov product suite, it is very easy to switch or migrate between product categories. All you have to do is simply follow the underlisted steps to migrate to your preferred product choice:

    • Identify the Motor product you wish to switch to
    • Request for a product migration form from our sales advisor
    • Complete and submit the migration form indicating your preferred product
    • Pay the additional premium as required
    • Your request will be processed subject to terms and conditions
    • You can begin to enjoy a new product category within 48 hours Is there provision for full or partial refund?

    Refund policy

    The policy is non-refundable

    Cover for non-owner driver

    Yes. Wapic covers the legal liability for loss or damage to someone else's property by any person who is driving, provided the y are fully licensed and operated the vehicle with your permission

    In case of an accident

    • Notify the Police immediately
    • Note the insurance details of the third party involved

    Moov Plus (Fire & Theft)

    This is a simple insurance product which covers the policyholder against legal liability to third parties in the event of death, bodily injury and property damage. Beyond these, this policy also covers theft or damage by fire. Under Moov Plus, the cost of repairs and/or outright replacement is also covered. The policy allows you to buy additional features such as personal accident and cover for riots along with the standard features of the product.

    Features

    • Third party property damage (up to N1million)
    • Third party bodily injury & death (Unlimited)
    • Covers fire damage to the insured vehicle
    • Covers theft of the vehicle
    • Covers authorised repair limit (Up to N10,000.00)
    • Includes towing limit (up to N10,000.00)
    • Passenger liability (N75,000 per person and a maximum of N350,000 per vehicle)
    • Policy deductibles/excess applies

    Benefits

    • Covers third party property damage
    • Covers third party bodily injury & death (Unlimited)
    • Covers cost of repairs caused by fire damage
    • Indemnifies in case of theft
    • No-Claim Bonus of 2.5% payable after 2nd year of renewal

    MARINE

    Marine Cargo Insurance provides indemnity for loss or damage to imported goods being conveyed by sea or air.

    Features

    • Ship being stranded, grounded, sunk or capsized
    • overturning or derailment of other land transport
    • collision of the ship or other form of transport with any external object
    • General Average & Jettison

    Benefits

    • Protects you from footing the bill of loss/damage of cargo
    • Provides unlimited cover for third party property loss
    • Cover is warehouse-to-warehouse; from the time the goods leave the seller to the place of storage at the final destination
    • Wapic offers an annual open cargo policy which automatically insures your shipments on set terms, conditions and rate without the need to contact us each time.

    To enjoy these benefits?

    • Original policy or certificate of insurance
    • Original or copy shipping invoice, together with shipping specification and/or weight notes.
    • Original Bill of Laden and/or other contract of carriage
    • Survey report or other documentary evidence to show the extent of the loss or damage
    • Landing account and weight notes at final destination.
    • Discrepancy certificate
    • Correspondence exchanged with the Carriers and other parties regarding their liability for the loss or damage

    GET A QUOTE

    Documentation

    • Copy of Form M
    • Country of origin / destination
    • Proforma Invoice
    • KYC Documentation

  • Home Owners
    X

    Home Owners Insurance

    Home Insurance is a comprehensive insurance that covers damage to a building occasioned by flood, fire and fallen trees as well as injury to visitors to the property. The product also covers the contents of a home such as furniture & fittings, jewelleries, refrigerator and so on.

    Features

    • Covers the loss of rent or the additional cost of alternative accommodation in case of fire
    • Covers replacement of stolen items (based on inventory provided)
    • Covers contents while temporarily removed for cleaning ,renovating, repairs or similar purpose.
    • Spouse is automatically considered an ‘Insured’ under this policy
    • Compensation for damage due to bursting or overflowing of water tanks and pipes

    Benefits

    • Indemnifies in the event of damage to the structures of the house.
    • Indemnifies in the event of loss due to burglary (theft) or fire
    • The policy will also cover you against liability for accidental injury to third parties or damage to their property that occurs on your premises
    • Can be extended to cover larceny (Theft not involving violence or forcible entry/exit is covered)

    To enjoy these benefits?

    • Witness statement / Security report (if any)
    • Purchase receipts / Invoice of stolen items
    • Quotation for the repairs of damage parts from a certified building contractor
    • Police Report (this is applicable if the claim is due to Burglary)
    • Fire brigade report (for fire claims) or Wapic appointed adjuster
    • Photographs of damaged properties or premises

    GET A QUOTE

    Documentation

    • Completed proposal form
    • Utility bill/Evidence of address
    • Record of inventories
    • Acceptable means of identification
