All Risk Insurance

This policy covers “All risks” of theft, loss or accidental damage to property...

Learn More

Read More

Product Brief:

This policy covers “All risks” of theft, loss or accidental damage to property insured subject to terms, exceptions and conditions of the policy. All-risks insurance is the most comprehensive type of coverage available. "All risks" means that any risk that the contract does not specifically omit is automatically covered.

Target Market:

Individual & Corporate

Benefits

  • Cover theft to the property insured
  • Cover fire, lighting and explosive to the property insured.
  • Cover accidental damage to the property Insured
  • It is suitable for expensive personal and /or commercial non heavy equipment moved frequently from one place to another.

Features

  • Provides financial compensation for items lost due to theft , fire, lighting and accidental damage
  • The cover is not restricted to particular premises or location where items are kept, but is operative within the specified geographical limit

Policy Documentation 

  • Completed Proposal Form
  • Utility bill
  • KYC: Certificate of incorporation, utility bill etc.
  • Placement/broking slip
  • Inventory of assets
  • Inventory of assets

Claims Documentation 

  • Duly completed claim form
  • Detailed statement on circumstance of loss
  • Statement of claim
  • Police report
