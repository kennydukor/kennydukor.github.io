Product Benefits

  • Return on Investment: Flexible but high return.
  • Accidental Medical Expenses (child): Up to N50,000 payable once in a year.
  • Academic Award (Best in Class): Up to 10% of your annual contribution, subject to a maximum of N20,000 (payable once)
  • Family Income Benefit: 10% of life cover paid at the anniversary of the policy to the family of the policyholder. Subject to no outstanding premium
  • Premium Waiver: Wapic takes up payment of contribution upon demise/permanent disability of the policyholder. Subject to no outstanding premium
  • Payable in the event of demise: Life cover + Family income Benefit + Premium Waiver + Investment account balance
  • Payable in the event of Permanent disability: Family income Benefit + Premium Waiver + Investment account balance
  • Premiums are tax deductible
